An ecosystem integrating education with earnings, created for an expanding cryptocurrency company. This project offers a real-time data dashboard for market tracking and a fun, engaging quiz module. The twist? Users get paid in crypto just for expanding their knowledge about crypto and financial markets. Learn, play, earn - a triple win!

Twitter Bot

A smart solution for staying ahead of the cryptocurrency market. This automated Twitter bot not only keeps its finger on the pulse of the crypto world but also shares the beat with followers. By scraping trending cryptocurrency news and delivering 2-4 updates per day, it ensures followers never miss a market movement.

Fuelling the growth of a dynamic heavy-duty mechanical service. The comprehensive 10+ page website has been designed to match the company's robust offerings. Seamlessly navigating through volumes of information, customers can quickly find services tailored to their needs.

A comprehensive and interactive website for a 24/7 Emergency Traffic Control and Sign Rentals business. The user-friendly interface and intuitive layout enable easy access to emergency services and sign rental bookings around the clock. The result is a digital platform that's always ready, just like their service.
